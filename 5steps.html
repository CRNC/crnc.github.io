5 Steps to More Reproducible Neuroimaging Research

Going whole hog into completely reproducible neuroimaging is hard. But, there are lots of little things you can do today to increase the reproducibility of your current studies.

Study Design

  • Build data shareability into the consent process
  • Plan your study with a RoadMap
  • Pre-register your study

Data Collection URL

  • Adopt standards-based data representation from the get go
    • Use ReproBIDS for your imaging data
    • Annotate your metadata as you collect it2
  • Use ReproIn to automate conversion to BIDS
  • Use a version control system for all files (data, code, environments, etc.)

Data Processing

  • Document your software and computational environment
  • Use containerization/virtualization to encapsulate and share analysis workflow (Docker, Singularity, Virtual Machines, etc.)
  • Annotate your results

Statistical Analysis

  • Most studies are underpowered, combat this by sharing your data and reusing available data.: Understand:
    • Effect size, confidence intervals, power, positive predictive value and significance testing

Publication Content

  • Include the anonymized raw data and workflow used
  • Include complete processed results
  • Make it Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable (FAIR)
